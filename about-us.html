Discussions and debates

Debate and Discussions is an initiative that helps children and teenagers with a holistic approach of development. Our motto is Open Skies. We focus on public speaking, English, descriptive and imaginative writing; through various engaging activities, games, ice-breakers and expand our knowledge by discussing quotes from a range of personalities. This initiative started in July, 2020, when the world was at a stop, but we weren't. Our program, uses online platforms Zoom and Google Meets. It is free of charge, and our vision is to enable skill sharing by elder kids to younger kids and help them grow into valuable members of our community.
